High School English Level 2 Vocabulary

Welcome to Quiz2-Website. This web page will help you study and understand the given vocabulary. Good luck!




expurgate

verb (used with object); to amend by removing words , passages, etc., deemed offensive or objectionable




acrimony

(noun); sharpness, harshness, or bitterness of nature , speech, disposition, etc.




illicit

(adjective); not legally permitted or authorized




qualitative

(adjective); pertaining to or concerned with quality or qualities




lax

(adjective); not strict or severe




inherent

(adjective); existing in someone or something as a permanent and inseparable element, quality, or attribute




admonish

verb(used with object); to caution, advise, or counsel against something
